Privacy Policy

Expense Rabbit ("the app") is a macOS application for tracking business travel expenses. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information we collect

Expense Rabbit collects only the information you provide directly within the app, including:

  • Expense records (amounts, categories, dates, notes)
  • Receipt images you attach to expenses
  • App settings and preferences

How your data is stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled, your data is synced across your devices via Apple's CloudKit service. We do not operate any external servers and do not have access to your data.

Third-party services

Expense Rabbit uses the following third-party service:

  • Frankfurter API — used to fetch live currency exchange rates. Only a request for the relevant currency pair is sent; no personal data is transmitted.

Data we do not collect

Expense Rabbit does not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Any data for advertising purposes

Children's privacy

Expense Rabbit is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
