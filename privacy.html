Privacy Policy

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how Expense Rabbit in Florida ("Company," "we," and "our") processes, collects, uses and shares personal data when using this App at www.expenserabbit.com (the "App"). Please read the following information carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we will process data.

Purposes of processing

What is personal data?

Within the context of this Policy, we collect various forms of information, encompassing what is commonly referred to as "personal data." As delineated in the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the term "personal data" pertains to any information that, either individually or when amalgamated with other data under our purview, serves to uniquely identify you as an individual. Such data may encompass, but is not restricted to, particulars such as your name, postal address, email address, and telephone number.

Why do we need your personal data?

Our processing of personal data adheres strictly to the provisions outlined in relevant data protection and privacy legislation. The collection of specific personal data is indispensable for the provision of access to our App. When you registered with us, you were prompted to provide your consent to furnish this information to facilitate access to our services, product acquisition, or content perusal. You retain the right to revoke this consent at any time. If you dissent with our utilization of your personal data in accordance with this Policy, please refrain from using our App.

Collecting your personal data

Information you give us

  • The personal data you provide when you register to use our App, including your name and email address
  • The personal data you provide when you report a problem with our App or when we provide you with customer support
  • The personal data you provide when you correspond with us by phone, email or otherwise

Information from social networking apps

Our App includes interfaces that allow you to connect with social networking sites (each a "SNS"). If you connect to a SNS through our App, you authorize us to access, use and store the information that you agreed the SNS could provide to us based on your settings on that SNS. You can revoke our access at any time by amending the appropriate settings from within your account settings on the applicable SNS.

Information automatically collected

We automatically log information about you and your device when you access our App — including operating system, browser type, screen resolution, pages viewed, time spent on pages, access times, and actions on our App. We collect this information using cookies.

Automated decision making and profiling

We do not use your personal data for the purposes of automated decision-making. However, we may do so in order to fulfill obligations imposed by law, in which case we will inform you and provide you with an opportunity to object.

Cookies

What are cookies?

We may collect information through the use of "cookies" — small data files stored on your device. We use session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain until you remove them) to provide you with an enhanced and personalized experience.

Two categories of cookies

  • First party cookies: Dispensed directly by us to recognize your device when you revisit our App.
  • Third party cookies: Administered by service providers operating on our App, which may identify your device on our App and elsewhere.

Sharing your personal data

  • Third parties designated by you. We may share your personal data with third parties where you have provided consent to do so.
  • Our third party service providers. We may share your personal data with providers who deliver services such as data analysis, payment processing, IT infrastructure, customer service, email delivery, and auditing.

Third party sites

Our App may contain links to third party apps and features. This Policy does not cover the privacy practices of such third parties. They have their own privacy policies and we do not accept any responsibility or liability for their apps, features or policies. Please read their privacy policies before submitting any data to them. One such app is RevenueCat — their privacy policy can be found on their website.

User generated content

You may share personal data with us when you submit user-generated content to our App. Please note that any information you post or disclose may become available to other users and to the general public. We urge you to be careful when deciding to disclose your personal data on our App.

It is prohibited to upload or transmit content through the App that includes abusive behavior, threats, harassment, bullying, graphic materials, sexually explicit materials, promotion of illicit substances, or any materials inconsistent with applicable law ("Objectionable Content"). We reserve the right to remove such content and, at our discretion, to disable or terminate accounts of repeat violators.

International data transfer

Your information may be transferred to, stored at, and processed by us outside the country in which you reside, where data protection regulations may not offer the same level of protection as in other parts of the world. By accepting this Policy, you agree to this transfer, storage or processing. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Policy.

Security

We seek to use reasonable organizational, technical and administrative measures to protect personal data within our organization. Unfortunately, no transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure. If you believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please contact us immediately.

Retention

We will only retain your personal data as long as reasonably required for you to use the App until you close your account or cancel your subscription, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Our policy on children

Our App is not directed to children under 16. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with information without their consent, please contact us. We will delete such information from our files as soon as reasonably practicable.

Your rights

  • Opt-out. You may contact us anytime to opt-out of direct marketing communications, automated decision-making, collection of sensitive personal data, new processing beyond the original purpose, or transfer of your data outside the EEA.
  • Access. You may access the information we hold about you at any time via your account or by contacting us directly.
  • Amend. You can contact us to update or correct inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Move. Your personal data is portable — you have the flexibility to move your data to other service providers as you wish.
  • Erase and forget. In certain situations, you can request that we erase your data when it is no longer relevant or is incorrect.

We will try to comply with your request as soon as reasonably practicable and in any event within one month. Please note that we may need to retain certain information for recordkeeping purposes.

Complaints

We are committed to resolving any complaints about our collection or use of your personal data. Please contact us and we will reply within 30 days. If you feel your complaint has not been adequately resolved, you reserve the right to contact your local data protection supervisory authority.
